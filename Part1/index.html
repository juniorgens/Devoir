À propos de moi

I hail from the vibrant landscapes of Cameroon, where I completed my primary and secondary education, laying a strong foundation for my academic journey. I am fluent in three languages—French, English, and Italian—allowing me to connect across cultures with ease.

Currently, I reside in Italy, pursuing a degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering at the prestigious University of Ferrara, where I am thriving in my third year. My passion for robotics and cutting-edge technologies drives me to embrace challenges, as they push me to explore my limits and surpass them.

Since March 2025, I have been immersed in an enriching academic exchange program at the Shibaura Institute of Technology in Japan. This transformative experience has broadened my intellectual horizons and expanded my global network, offering me fresh perspectives and invaluable connections.
