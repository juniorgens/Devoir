À propos de moi

I’m originally from Cameroon, where I completed both my primary and secondary education — first at People Progressive Bilingual School, and later at Collège De La Salle. Currently, I live in Italy, where I’m pursuing a degree in Electronic and Computer Engineering at the University of Ferrara. I'm now in my third year of studies. I’ve always been passionate about robotics and emerging technologies. I enjoy taking on challenges because they allow me to push my limits and grow, both personally and academically. Since March, I’ve been in Japan as part of an exchange program at Shibaura Institute of Technology, which has been an exciting opportunity to explore new perspectives and broaden my international experience.
